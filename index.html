Saudi Arabia Information:

Saudi Arabia is located in the Middle East. Saudi Arabia is bordered
by and the Red Sea to the west, Yemen and Oman to the south, the Arabian Gulf, Qatar and
United Arab Emirates to the east, and Kuwait, Iraq, and Jordan to the north.

Explore Saudi Arabia Using Google Earth:

Google Earth is a free
program from Google that allows you to explore satellite images showing
the cities and landscapes of Saudi Arabia and all of Asia in fantastic
detail. It works on your desktop computer, tablet, or mobile phone. The
images in many areas are detailed enough that you can see houses,
vehicles and even people on a city street. Google Earth is free and easy-to-use.

Saudi Arabia on a World Wall Map:

Saudi Arabia is one of nearly 200
countries illustrated on our Blue Ocean Laminated Map of the World. This

great map for students, schools, offices and anywhere that a nice map of
